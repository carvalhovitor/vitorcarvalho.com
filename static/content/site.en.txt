Title: Vitor Carvalho

----

Description: Vitor is an independent designer developing printed and digital works for individuals and institutions in the cultural and commercial sectors. He designs with a focus on building systems that combine typography and careful use of materials, taking into consideration the specificities of each project. Vitor holds a bachelor's degree in Graphic Design from UEMG, with one year of studies at Parsons School of Design.

----

Location: Belo Horizonte, Brazil
