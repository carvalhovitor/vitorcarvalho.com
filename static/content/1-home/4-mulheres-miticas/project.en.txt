Title: Mulheres Míticas

----

Text: 

The book Mulheres Míticas em Performance celebrates the theatre group Mulheres Míticas’ sixth anniversary with the publication of two plays and multiple related essays and interviews. The graphic project uses visual assets found in archives to organize the different types of text, underlining the book as part of the group’s living memory. The cover and back cover reveal the content along with all of the object’s technicalities.

14 × 21 cm
320 pages
